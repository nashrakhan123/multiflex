DUNKI

In 2020, an aged Manu Randhawa runs away from a hospital in London. She meets an immigration lawyer, Puru Patel, and asks him to connect her with Hardayal "Hardy" Singh Dhillon, her ex-lover. In Laltu, Punjab, an aged Hardy is ecstatic to receive Manu's call after 25 years. She asks him to meet her in Dubai, so that he can bring her back to India through the "Dunki" route. In 1995, Manu and her friends, Buggu and Balli, live in Laltu, and yearn to immigrate to London for a better life. Poor and uneducated, they are unable to procure a visa, despite trying various methods. One day, Hardy, an ex-Army officer, arrives in their village.
